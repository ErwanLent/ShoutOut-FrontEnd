Media Assets

ShoutOut is a location based chatting app coming soon to iPhone, iPad, and Android. Users can create chatrooms by pinning it on the map, specifying a radius and keywords. People can join chatrooms which they are physically in range of and communicate. You can also make / join global or password protected chatrooms.

Living in Isla Vista (UCSB college town) one of the biggest party destinations in the world, we noticed many people were having trouble staying connected and finding the best parties. Culture here is to throw a huge party and let anyone join, however often times there is a disconnect between people throwing parties and people searching for parties.

Shoutout makes it easy for people to share the best parties and for students to find out where they are. The uses of shoutout go well beyond parties though. People can form study groups, find out where the best places are to eat in a new city, connect at concerts and live events, or just form private group chats with their friends for free. You can private message users, add friends, stay anonymous or share your identity.

Spam will be prevented through crowd sourced bans and suspension. You can report users and after a certain number of reports have been received for the same user, the user will be banned, or suspended from the chatroom. Repeat offenses will result in a series of suspension / bans of increased detriment.

Users will find the most relevant chatrooms by seeing the nearest ones on a map, searching by keywords, and chatroom invites from their friends.

ShoutOut is coming to iOS and Android.
